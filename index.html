About Us

Mandara's run by Franccho Mandara, cozy atmosphere, a true gastronomic journey.

The products offered are manufactured in-house, and for that we have a central that supplies all units in the network.

All items are handcrafted with low fat content. The pasta is made with organic eggs, durum semolina and special flour. The absence of fried foods and the monitoring of nutritionists guarantee the taste, quality and safety of all products in the store and restaurant.
